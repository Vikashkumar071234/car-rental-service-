About us

We reimagine the way the world moves for better

Movement is what we power.it's our lifeblood.it's what gets us out of bed each morning.it pushes us to constantly reimagine how we can move better.For you.For all the places you want to go.For all the things you want to get.Across the entire world.In real time.At the incredible speed now.

Sustainability

vvs is committing to become a fully electric,zero-emission platform by 2040,with 100% of rides taking place in zero-emissionvechiles,on public transit,or with microbility.it is our responsibility as the largest mobility platform in the world to more aggressively tackle the challenge of climate change.we will do this by offering riders more ways to ride green,helping drivers to go electric,making transparency a priority and parterning with NGOs and the private sector to help expedite a clean and just energy transition.

Rides and beyond

In addition to helping riders find a way to go from point A to point B,we're helping people to travel in less amount ,removing barriers to healthcare,creating new freight-booking solutions,and helping companies provide a seamless employee travel experience.And always helping drivers and couriers earn.

Your safety drives us

Whether you are in the back seat or behind the wheel,your safety is essential.we are committed to doing our part ,and technology is at the heart of our approach.we partner with safety advocates and develop new technologies and systems to help improve and help make it easier for everyone to get around.
